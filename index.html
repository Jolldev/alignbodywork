Treatment Options

Swedish Massage

Therapeutic relaxation techniques which include long, gliding strokes, rolling, kneading & percussive techniques to improve circulation and decrease pain, stress levels and muscle adhesion.

Sports Massage

Bodywork utilizing various techniques targeted toward athletic clients. It is used to speed up the healing of injuries, injury prevention, prepare the body for athletic activity and help muscles function properly to maximize performance.

Craniosacral Massage

A gentle, but powerful modality to aide in the treatment of headaches, chronic pain, IBS & anxiety. The practitioner uses light touch to assist the natural movement of cerebrospinal fluid within the craniosacral system.

Deep Tissue

Bodywork of the fascia and deeper layers of connective tissue in the body using slow, sustained & deep pressure at problem areas to relieve tension. Recommended for those with chronic stress, injuries and active individuals. Soreness can be common after this type of bodywork and drinking lots of water is essential to help with the removal of toxins released & recovery.

Precision Neuromuscular Therapy

Slow, sustained pressure or vibration techniques to release trigger points, also known as areas of tissue strain, in the body. It is used to locate & release spasms, decrease referred pain, improve circulation & help to restore proper postural alignment & connective tissue flexibility.

Gua Sha

Ancient Chinese healing technique that involves scraping your skin with a massage tool to stimulate new oxygenated blood flow to the areas, break up connective tissue adhesion and relieve tension in the fascia and superficial muscles.
