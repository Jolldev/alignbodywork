About Me

My name is Sara Friske. I am the owner and Licensed Massage Therapist of Align Bodywork. I received training at Blue Sky School of Professional Massage & Therapeutic Bodywork in their Medical Intensive Track. I am also a Physical Therapist Assistant, working primarily in acute and subacute patient care, since 2014. This combination of training and experience enhances my knowledge and skillset of human anatomy and physiology, medical conditions and provides a different perspective on the treatment of pain and enhancing function.

Personally, I am very passionate about nutrition, physical activity and all thing’s wellness. I am an ultramarathon runner, dabble in powerlifting/bodybuilding, love to road bike, hike, travel and just spend time with my husband and dogs.

I believe in utilizing a holistic approach to assist the body to function to its full capabilities. Via collaboration with you and my training and experience, I focus to:

  • Restore function and encourage healing in areas of soft tissue restriction and trauma throughout the entire body that do, or have the ability to, cause pain, movement dysfunction and poor performance
  • Help the body and mind relax, reset and restore energy
  • Assist in improving function of the circulatory and lymphatic systems to promote the body’s natural healing

I look forward to working with you and developing a treatment plan together to keep your body, mind, and passions aligned.
