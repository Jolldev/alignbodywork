About Me

My name is Sara Friske and I am the owner of Align Bodywork. I am currently receiving training at Blue Sky School of Professional Massage & Therapeutic Bodywork in their Medical Intensive Track. I am also a Physical Therapist Assistant, working primarily in acute and subacute patient care, since 2014. This combination of training and experience enhances my knowledge and skillset of human anatomy and physiology, medical conditions, and provides a different perspective on the treatment of pain and enhancing function.

Personally, I am very passionate about nutrition, physical activity, and all things wellness. I am an ultramarathon runner, dabble in powerlifting & bodybuilding, love to road bike, hike, travel and just spend time with my husband and dogs.
